How It Works
  • Submit your design — upload reference images, BrickLink files (.ldr/.io/.mpd), or 3D models.
  • We review it — our team evaluates accuracy, buildability, and market fit within 2–3 weeks.
  • We produce it — if selected, we handle manufacturing, packaging, and instruction manuals.
  • You earn — 15% of every set sold goes directly to you, paid monthly.

What We're Looking For
  • Historical accuracy — correct markings, proportions, and period-appropriate details.
  • Buildability — must be constructable from real bricks with a clear build sequence.
  • Market gap — vehicles or scenes not yet in our catalog get priority.
  • Any skill level — beginners to expert builders all welcome.
